We built this app because we've been there. The grueling journey of the CA exams, where less than 10% make it through on the first attempt.

As a Chartered Accountant who conquered it in one attempt, I saw too many peers stumble, not because they lacked knowledge, but because they lacked a clear plan.

The stress, the chaos, the endless hours, it doesn't have to be that way. We started this to give every CA student a free, simple way to chart their path, connect with those who've won, and find the focus to succeed.

It's free forever. No ads, no sign-ups. It's our way of supporting the next generation of CAs.
